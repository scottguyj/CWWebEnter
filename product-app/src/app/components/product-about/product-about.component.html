ABOUT THIS APP

This web app is to allow a user to keep track of food and drink products that may go out of date without the user realising. The app will store the name, location(where the product is stored), the expiry date and the amount of the product the user has stored. Along with being abole to store each product, the user will obviously be able to look at all the products that they currently have stored. If you use a product, you can delete the product from the list or edit the product information to change the amount you have left od the product. The aim of this app is to reduce the amount of waste that the average household produces. This app follows a MEAN stack which is MongoDB, Express, Angular and Node.js
